Latin Correction

Our system is specialized for early 16th century Latin manuscripts, focusing on transcription errors while staying close to the original text. It preserves period-specific abbreviations and spelling characteristics while making necessary corrections.

Dutch Translation

The translation creates accessible, friendly Dutch that modern readers can easily engage with while maintaining fidelity to the original Latin meaning and tone. It preserves the warmth and personality of the original correspondence.

About This Project

This web application is designed to help scholars, researchers, and enthusiasts working with early 16th century Latin manuscripts. It combines advanced AI technology with specialized knowledge of historical Latin texts to provide accurate corrections and translations.

The output is formatted in an elegant three-column A3 landscape layout that simulates facing pages in a book, with Latin text and Dutch translation properly centered on their respective A4 areas, separated by an appropriate gutter.

This tool is particularly useful for archives, universities, libraries, and individual researchers working with historical Latin correspondence and documents.
